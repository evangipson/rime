Session #2

  • The party tracked the verbeeg down to a cave about 2 hours outside of Goodmead
  • The party goes into the verbeeg lair and fights a verbeeg and a bear
  • The bear kind of fucks up the entire party
    • Sven and Brynn get knocked down
    • Chroneer casts sleep on the bear to save the fight
    • Sven, Chroneer, and Pillby stab the bear in the neck together and murder it
  • The party takes a short rest to recover
  • The party finds a tomb in the verbeeg cave
  • The party discovers a cheiftan holding a pearl orb and a wand, which are both magical, which Chroneer takes out of curiosity
    • The pearl was a Pearl of Power, which goes to Pillby
    • The wand was a Wand of the Warmage
  • Chroneer sees a vision of an undead face
  • The party fills up their canteens with water from a pool in the verbeeg cave
    • Upon drinking the water, Chroneer's curse is dispelled
  • The party finds:
    • 72 silver, gets distributed to the party
    • 344 copper, gets distributed to the party
    • translucent pink moss agate gemstone, goes to Pillby
    • healer's kit, goes to Sven
    • hunting trap, goes to Sven
    • small rabbitskin quiver containing 10 silvered arrows, goes to Sven
    • A basket of precious otherworldly metals (worth 75gp)
  • The party encounters a verbeeg, which Brynn fools by disguising himself while the party sneaks away with a cart of barrels of mead
  • The party heads back to Goodmead with the cart, which takes 3 hours
  • Allevesa asks Finnegan to become the speaker of Goodmead, which Finnegan denies
    • Shandar listens in to the conversation
  • The party takes a long rest in Goodmead, after which Chroneer discusses a plan to use sleep on the last remaining verbeeg and interrogate her
  • The party navigates back to the verbeeg cave, and encounters the remaining verbeeg
    • Sven is downed
  • Sven downs the last remaining verbeeg without getting to interrogate her
  • The party decides to go to the verbeeg cave to find concrete evidence of Sephek's influence
    • The party also suspects Shandar of working with the verbeeg, as he's running for speaker of Goodmead
    • The party finds more precious otherworldly metals (worth 75gp)
    • The trail is cold, no additional tracks
    • The party notices the bodies are moved around
    • Chroneer discovered the verbeeg moved the bodies around
  • The party goes back to Goodmead, and asks for the whereabouts of Torga's Caravan, and they're in Dougan's Hole
